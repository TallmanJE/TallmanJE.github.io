Horoscope Hunters

Horoscope Hunters are a dedicated group of students delving into the Asrological lifestyle. Over the course of a semester Nicole, Paige, Kate, and I have been writing newsletters, recorded a podcast, and made a video on the subject of Astrology. Below is our work, enjoy.

Podcast

In this podcast we interview a beginner, an intermidiate astrologist, and a couple who are experts on the subject. Through asking them questions about their relationships with the stars we learned that astrology can impact your life on any level.

Video

Our video maps the star chart of celebrity Kim Kardashian. Using gems we highlight the qualities of her birth chart in relation to her life. As a group, we thought it would teach others how to read their birthcharts. Also, we hoped it could serve as a tool for others to get more in-tune with themselves.

Newsletters

Throughout the semster I wrote three newsletters. The first newsletter, Astronomy vs Astrology...Place Your Bets, dives into the differences between the science and the concept of astrology. The second newsletter, Wait...What's My Sign?, explores the shift in astrological charts with the inclusion of a sign that was abandoned thousands of years ago. The final newsletter, Stars For Sale- What Not To Fall For in The Astrology Business, is a Dos and Don'ts guide for those exploring astrology. Other newsletters from my group are linked below.

The 12 Signs

Here are the twelve astrological signs with focuses on their strength and weaknesses. The strengths and weaknesses of each sign are a main focus in many of our projects as Horoscope Hunters.

Pisces

The strengths pisces signs hold are compassion, wisdom, and musical orientation. Weaknesses of the sign include being overly trusting, sadness, and the desire to escapse reality.

Aries

The strengths aries signs hold are determination, confidence, and optimism. Weaknesses of the sign include impatience, moodiness, and being impulsive.

Taurus

The strengths taurus signs hold are patience, devotion, and stability. Weaknesses of the sign include stuborness, possessiveness, and not being able to compromise.

Gemini

The strengths gemini signs hold are curiousity, affection, and the ability to learn quickly and exchange ideas. Weaknesses of the sign include nervousness, incinsistency, and indecisiveness.

Cancer

The strengths cancer signs hold are tenacity, loyalty, and persuasiveness. Weaknesses of the sign include moodiness, suspiciousness, and being insecure.

Leo

The strengths leo signs hold are creativity, generosity, and cheerfulness. Weakness of the sign include arrogance, being self-centered, and inflexibility.

Virgo

The strengths virgo signs hold are kindness, practicality, and being analytical. Weaknesses of the sign include shyness, worrying, and having an all work no play attitude.

Libra

The strengths libra signs hold are diplomacy, being social, and graciousness. Weaknesses of the sign include carrying a grudge, self-pity, and avoiding confrontations.

Scorpio

The strengths scorpio signs hold are braveness, passion, and resourcefulness. Weaknesses of the sign include jealousy, violence, and being secretive.

Sagittarius

The strengths sagittarius signs hold are generosity, being idealistic, and having a great sense of humor. Weaknesses of the sign include being very impatient, saying anything no matter how undiplomatic, and promising more than they can deliver.

Capricorn

The strengths that capricorn signs hold are responsibility, discipline, and self-control. Weaknesses of the sign include being kinow-it-alls, expecting the worst, and being condescending.

Aquarius

The strengths that aquarius signs hold are orginality, being progressive, and being humanitatians. Weaknesses of the signs include being tempermental, not compromising, and running from emotional expression.
